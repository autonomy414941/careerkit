Salary To Hourly Formula Guide

Use this formula to convert annual salary into an hourly value that reflects your actual schedule.

Core Formula

Hourly rate = Annual salary / Effective worked hours per year

Where:

  • Effective worked hours = (hours per week x working weeks) - (paid days off x hours per day)
  • Hours per day = hours per week / 5 for a standard five-day week

Example

Assume salary is $92,000, schedule is 40 hours/week, working weeks are 52, and paid days off are 15.

  • Hours per day = 40 / 5 = 8
  • Effective worked hours = (40 x 52) - (15 x 8) = 1,960
  • Hourly rate = 92,000 / 1,960 = $46.94

This rate is usually higher than salary/2,080 because paid days off lower worked hours.

Assumption Checklist

  • Set working weeks to your real work year, not always 52.
  • Adjust paid days off for holidays and employer policy changes.
  • If your schedule is not five days per week, recalculate hours per day directly.
  • For contractor benchmarking, add overhead for benefits and unpaid gaps.
